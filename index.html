2. Why We Exist

The agricultural industry in Cyprus faces several challenges, including employee management, high costs, and limited technology adoption. We aim to address these issues by developing AI-driven solutions that are affordable, scalable, and targeted to meet the specific needs of small to medium-sized farms.

Market Pain Points

Employee Management Issues:

  • One of the biggest challenges observed in farms, especially in Cyprus, is the management of farm labor. The work is often viewed as undesirable, leading to high turnover rates and a lack of motivation among employees. Farmers frequently struggle with ensuring that necessary tasks are completed efficiently and reliably, as employees often lack the drive to perform at the required standard.

High Costs and Affordability:

  • While advanced agricultural technologies exist, they are typically designed for large-scale operations and come with high costs that are prohibitive for small to medium-sized farms. These smaller farms, particularly those that are run as profitable businesses rather than side-hustles, are looking for affordable, scalable solutions that can improve their operations without the need for significant upfront investment.

Limited Scope of Existing Technologies:

  • Current agricultural technologies often focus on broad solutions that do not address specific issues. For example, many existing systems fail to provide precise monitoring of animal welfare during critical stages, such as calving, which is vital for the health and productivity of livestock.

Operational Inefficiencies:

  • Farmers often face the burden of constant supervision and manual monitoring. This challenge is exacerbated by varying farm sizes and the lack of affordable, effective, and targeted technology, resulting in many tasks being performed manually, which leads to inefficiencies and potential losses.

Resistance to Technology Adoption:

  • While there is a growing interest in agricultural technology, smaller farms, especially those run as side-hustles, may be hesitant to adopt new technologies due to perceived complexity or a lack of understanding of the benefits. This makes it essential to target farms that are run as serious, profitable businesses and to provide clear, tangible value propositions.

3. Our Solution

Introducing Our AI Solutions: We aim to revolutionize farming by developing a suite of AI-driven products that are designed to meet the needs of farms of various sizes that are focused on profitability and operational efficiency. Our solutions are not just about introducing new technology; they are tailored to complement existing systems, focusing on solving specific, often overlooked issues that are critical to farm operations. Our AI-driven solutions are affordable, scalable, and designed to address the unique challenges that farms face today—challenges such as effective employee management, precise monitoring of livestock, and reducing the burden of constant supervision. By targeting these pain points, we enable farmers to enhance efficiency, improve animal welfare, and reduce operational costs, all in a cost-effective manner that integrates seamlessly with their current operations.

Calv Alert - Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring with Live Stream:

  • Continuous video feed analysis with the ability to view live footage through the app, allowing farmers to visually confirm situations as they happen.

Immediate, Customizable Alerts:

  • Instant notifications sent via the mobile app, SMS, or other messaging systems, with options for farmers to customize and manage alert settings.
  • For those less familiar with technology, we offer additional options such as phone call alerts or specialized equipment like a dedicated tablet or receiver for notifications.
  • All information can be localized to ensure ease of use in different regions and languages.

Non-Invasive Technology:

  • No physical devices are attached to animals, minimizing discomfort and reducing the risk of infection.

Scalability:

  • Capable of monitoring multiple calving pens simultaneously, making it suitable for farms of all sizes.

User-Friendly App Interface:

  • An intuitive app that supports multiple users, enabling farm workers and veterinarians to easily receive alerts, view live feeds, and monitor calving signs.

Optional Data Storage and Analysis:

  • Provides the option to store video footage and detection data for later review, helping farmers analyze patterns over time.

Collaboration with Smyrillis Jersey Farm:

We are already in collaboration with Smyrillis Jersey Farm, one of the most technologically advanced farms in Cyprus, to test and perfect our Calv Alert system. This partnership provides a valuable opportunity to refine our product in a real-world setting, with the potential to expand our collaboration once the product proves successful.
